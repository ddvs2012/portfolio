About Me...




I'm Danny from Atlanta and this is my humble website.. I've lived all over the place, East to West; from Manila to Marin County. There's nothing better than exploring new places and trying new things. While seeing and living in all these different places definitely has its appeal, I'm ready to make Atlanta my homebase.


This is my fiance Fiona!
She works as a school teacher in Hong Kong, so we have the entire world and all of it's oceans between us, but that isn't enough to keep us apart.

Sooner or later we'll be together again, that's what keeps me going. The internet helps us talk and see each other, and I feel an overwhelming sense of gratitude to everyone who makes it possible. It's why I want to be a part of it, it's something I believe in.


If there is something that you like about the site, or something you don't, please let me know about it! Also if you see something here that you'd like to reproduce on your site, feel free to ask me, I'd be happy to help you out. Drop me an email or shoot me a text here. Thanks for reading!
